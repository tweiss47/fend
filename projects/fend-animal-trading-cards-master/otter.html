River Otter

River otters are known for their sense of play, which includes wrestling and chasing.

  • Scientific Name: Lontra canadensis
  • Average Weight: 11.5 kilograms
  • Average Lifespan: 21 years
  • Habitat: Inland waterways and costal areas

Short and stocky with a thick coat of brown fur. River otters range throughout North America and Europe. And are darn cute.
